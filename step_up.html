Step Up

Problem Statement:

Majority of students from Singapore Institue of Technology (SIT) frequently use the Resource Booking System (RBS) to book facilities and equipment. However, our research has ascertained that there are multiple pain points that affect the overall bookning experience for the students.
Problem 1: Unable to filter
Problem 2: Cluttered Interface
Problem 3: Not responsive on mobile

Project Objective:

The objective of this research is to improve the overall student life experience when using the SIT RBS in Singapore Polytechnic by making a user friendly and intuitive mobile app.


Project Scope

The team will gather user feedbacks for the current RBS such as the user's profile, pain points, experience, suggestions and improvements. The team will also research on other booking systems out on the market and use it as references for this project.
