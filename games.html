Student project works

Neor

Neor is a 2D Narrative Platformer that explores the 1st stage of grief; denial. Neor denies the loss of his loved one, Khloee, and shuns help from his fellow villagers, not wanting to believe that Khloee is gone. Neor's inner state is depicted through the breaking apart of the map, insinuating the effects of what long-term denial can do to someone as he slowly begins to accept the truth on his journey.

Genre 2D, Narrative, Platformer

Pave

Pave is a 2D Top-down, turn-based strategy combat, Medieval Fantasy RPG Game. The idea is to defeat enemies and pave your way to freedom using Tetrominoes. Pave is a game inspired by turn-based strategy, Medieval Fantasy and Tetris games.

Genre 2D, Narrative, turn-based

Disinheritance

Disinheritance is a 3D First-person, horror-stealth game where you play as a vengeful thief determined to steal artworks made by your renowed uncle as your rightful claim to your inheritance.

Genre 3D, Horror, Stealth
